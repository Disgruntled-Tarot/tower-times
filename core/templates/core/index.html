The Tower Times

I was raised by a father who came from Mexico with nothing but grit and a warning: “Don’t rely on the government. They’ll change the rules when it benefits them.”

I believed in hard work—so much that I gave it everything. My time, my health, my relationships. I didn’t watch the system collapse. I lived inside it while it quietly folded around me.

Loss, debt, burnout, and emotional silence became familiar terrain. But when the system stopped making sense, I realized: it was working exactly as designed—just not for people like me.

The Tower Times is for anyone waking up in the middle of the storm—still here, still aware, and ready to see what lies beyond the illusion.
